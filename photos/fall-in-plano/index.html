Fall in Plano

Photos taken in Plano, TX on November 11, 2019 with Sony A7rII, Nikon Coolpix P1000

Pictures from a fall walk along the Chisholm Trail in Plano, Texas with Kassi and the boy one day past the absolute peak of fall color. Kassi had a great writeup about it a couple days ago where she demonstrated and gave her impressions of the camera on the new iPhone 11 Pro.
