Photos from the [Magadalena Bay](https://imaginaldisk.world) Imaginal Disk concert in Dallas on October 5th with Lu. Great story or concept album. Lucy filled me in on all the lore, which is pretty trippy in a Cronenberg/Lynchian kind of way. It was my first time at the House of Blues. It's a great music venue for smaller, more intimate shows. The bar could use some work, though. The margaritas were embarrassing.
